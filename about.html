About Me

Designer, Coder, Learner

Hello! My name is Yiling. I'm currently a master's student at the University of Texas at Austin's School of Information, learning user experience and data analysis, and I'm always interested in how technology can be used to benefit society.

I am passionate about sustainability. I volunteered to help a grassroots Indian NGO build a content management system so they could share their movements for equity. I also worked for a traditional Taiwanese soy sauce business, where I managed eco-friendly product development and established brewing process management systems, allowing local farmers to expand their environmentally friendly land. I discovered that I learned a lot from being in a diverse environment, so I appreciate diversity.

I am a curious person who enjoys learning and exploring. Hiking, traveling, meditation, and playing the piano are some of my favorite hobbies.
